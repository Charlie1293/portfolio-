Chilli oil noodle recipe

for my recipe,I will teach you how to cook chili oil noodles.It is a simple recipe that takes less then 20 minutes to make.These noodles orignated in Shaanxi Province, China.They are called "You Po Mian" in chinese.They are thousands of years old. Tradiontally the noodles are made freshly from wheat,flour, and eggs.

    Directions

  1. Start off by boiling some water.
  2. place the noodles in the boiling water for about 5-10 minutes.
  3. while the noodles are boiling, put soy sauce,chili oil,black pepper, green onions, and garlic in a bowl.
  4. when the noodles are done boiling, drain the water from the pot.
  5. on a pan, heat up a little bit oil for about 2 minutes.
  6. mix the hot oil slowly with the combination in the bowl.
  7. Finally, add the noodles in and you have it!
ingredient amount
garlic 2-3 tablespoon
soy sauce 3-4 tablespoons
chilli oil 2-3 tablespoons
black pepper to taste
hot oil personal prefrence
